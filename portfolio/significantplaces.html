Significant Places

2018

2018 was the year of quick, quirky, and fun mappy gifts!

Tools:

  • Esri ArcGIS Pro

Data:

  • Mapstyler (Esri UK App)
  • ArcGIS Vector Tile Style Editor
  • Historic Aerials

My end gift was a frame with the following images of significant places in my family members' life.


To make them, I began with choosing nine special locations to my family member. I played with different shapes and designs (e.g. hexagons, triangles, etc.), but enjoyed the simplicity of nine uniform circles.



For this one, below, I used the SUPER handy Esri UK App Mapstyler to generate color schemes that match with my recipient's home.



For this one, below, I used the new ArcGIS Vector Tile Style Editor and bought historical aerials from Historic Aerials. I then mixed and matched the two different styles.
